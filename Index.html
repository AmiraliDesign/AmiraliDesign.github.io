About Me

With over 8 years of experience in brand identity design, I help businesses and organizations articulate their unique stories through thoughtful visual systems. My approach combines strategic thinking with meticulous craftsmanship to create identities that are both distinctive and enduring.

I believe great design should be invisible—it should feel inevitable, not imposed. My work is rooted in research, collaboration, and a deep understanding of how visual language shapes perception.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring typography history, photographing urban details, or perfecting my pour-over coffee technique.
